🎲 What is BeirutPoly?

BeirutPoly is a Monopoly-style board game inspired by the vibrant city of Beirut. Buy, trade, and upgrade famous streets, cultural spots, and chaotic corners while surviving blackouts, traffic jams, and political scandal cards!
